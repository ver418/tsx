Raise (RAISE)

A future stability layer concept. Off-protocol for now — earned, not promised. TSX ships first. Raise comes later when the foundation is proven.

What Raise is (and what it isn’t)

Raise is planned as a stability-focused token that may eventually support everyday tax-planning workflows. It is intentionally not marketed as a “guaranteed peg” or a hype stablecoin.

  • Not live at launch. TSX launches first and proves fee-powered sustainability.
  • No custody. Users control their own wallets at all times.
  • No promises. No “always $1” claims. No yield claims. No shortcuts.
TSX first Raise later Earned stability Transparency-first

Why it exists

The long-term goal is to help everyday people handle taxes with less stress: saving, planning, and smoothing volatility without hype or leverage.

  • Designed to be understandable and boring — in a good way.
  • Built around clear rules, public reporting, and simple UX goals.
  • Aligned with TSX’s mission: sustainability and protection.

How it could be supported

If Raise is ever launched, support would come from disciplined treasury behavior and external asset strategy — not from promises, not from printing, and not from leverage.

  • Potential support from stable reserves / external assets strategy.
  • Potential integrations with savings and tax-workflow tools.
  • Clear, public disclosures — “show the receipts.”

Launch posture (locked)

Raise is intentionally off-protocol for now. We do not ship it until TSX’s fee system and transparency model are stable in real markets.

This page exists so visitors understand the roadmap: Raise is a future layer, not a launch token and not a “stablecoin promise.”

Next steps

  • Publish disclosures, definitions, and constraints (no hype language).
  • Ship Docs and Community pages for transparency and communication.
  • Keep Raise as a roadmap item until TSX proves sustainability.
