Tokenomics

Supply

Fixed supply: 100 trillion TSX minted once at genesis. No inflationary minting.

Base fee

3% transaction fee designed to fund the ecosystem without pricing people out.

  • 1.0% → Treasury
  • 1.0% → Stability & External Assets (BTC/SOL/stables/ETFs/gold/silver)
  • 0.5% → Controlled re-circulation
  • 0.5% → Creator (operations + personal income without dumping)

Launch protections

  • 90-day max transaction cap to reduce whale/bot dominance early.
  • Adaptive fees can temporarily rise (up to ~10%) during attacks with transparent admin control.

Deflation goal

Long-term deflation through buybacks & burns, funded by real activity.

Positioning

No profit promises. No hype. Utility-first framing and transparency-first posture.
