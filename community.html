Community

What the TSX community is

The TSX community is built around long-term thinking, transparency, and real-world utility — not hype cycles or short-term speculation.

Participation is open, but expectations are clear: respect the mission, the structure, and the people involved.

Core principles

  • No hype-driven promotion
  • No guaranteed returns or promises
  • Transparency over marketing
  • Utility first, price second
  • Long-term sustainability over short-term pumps
Transparency-first No buy buttons No leverage No hype cycles

How participation works

Early on, community participation is informational and observational. As the protocol matures, structured governance and feedback channels may be introduced deliberately — not rushed.

Until then, the focus is education, clarity, and alignment.

What this is not

  • Not a meme community
  • Not a promise of profit
  • Not a fast-launch DAO
  • Not an influencer-driven project
